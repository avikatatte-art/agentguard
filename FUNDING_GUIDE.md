## üöÄ **Alternative Funding Paths**

### **1. Bootstrapped**
- Pros: Full control, no dilution
- Cons: Slower growth
- Best if: Strong early revenue

### **2. Accelerator (Y Combinator)**
- Pros: Network, credibility, $500K
- Cons: 7% equity
- Best if: Need validation + network

### **3. Revenue-Based Financing**
- Pros: No dilution, fast
- Cons: Expensive (15-20% APR)
- Best if: Strong revenue, need bridge

### **4. Strategic Investment**
- Pros: Partnership + capital
- Cons: Potential conflicts
- Best if: Clear strategic fit (Datadog, PagerDuty)
